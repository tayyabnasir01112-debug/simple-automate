# SimpleAutomate Help Center

## Onboarding
1. **Verify your email** – The first signup email activates your workspace. Lost the link? Visit `/verify-email` and resend.
2. **Invite teammates** – In Settings → Workspace, invite colleagues so pipelines stay in sync.
3. **Customize pipeline** – Rename stages to match your sales process. Drag contacts through the Kanban board.

## CRM Tips
- **Notes** – Write markdown, preview before saving, and browse the revision timeline.
- **Tasks** – Assign follow-ups with due dates. Cron reminders email you 24h before.
- **Segments** – Tag contacts to build campaign audiences instantly.

## Marketing Automation
- **Automation builder** – Mix send-email, delay, tag, and move-stage actions. Use analytics cards on the Automations tab to monitor success/fail counts.
- **Campaigns** – Draft newsletters, choose recipients, schedule, and review delivery analytics (sent/opened/clicked).
- **Templates** – Save favorite messages once and reuse everywhere.

## Billing
- Pricing is £5/mo via Stripe Checkout. Click “Start your free trial” on `/pricing` for a 7-day trial. Billing status appears in Settings.

## Getting Support
- Email `hello@simpleautomate.co.uk`
- Use the in-app “Need help?” links on the dashboard or visit `/guide` for quick steps.
